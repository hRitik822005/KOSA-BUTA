About KOSA BUTA

KOSA BUTA is more than just a saree brand—it's a movement to revive, preserve, and share the rich heritage of Kosa silk weaving, rooted deeply in the cultural heart of Champa, Chhattisgarh, India. As proud custodians of a centuries-old art form, we are committed to bringing the world the finest Kosa silk sarees, handcrafted with passion, tradition, and creativity.

Our Legacy

The story of KOSA BUTA begins in the looms of Chhattisgarh, where the ancient tradition of weaving Kosa silk is passed down through generations. Revered for its natural golden sheen, strength, and breathability, Kosa silk is a fabric that holds a special place in Indian textile heritage. Every saree we create is a living representation of this tradition—painstakingly woven by skilled artisans who pour their soul into each thread.

At KOSA BUTA, we honor this legacy by combining traditional techniques with contemporary design sensibilities, creating sarees that are not only elegant and authentic but also relevant to modern lifestyles. From festive wear to everyday drapes, our sarees speak the language of grace, identity, and culture.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to make beautiful, high-quality Kosa silk sarees accessible to all. We focus on affordability without compromising on authenticity or craftsmanship. Each design reflects our dedication to detail, our love for Indian culture, and our desire to empower artisans and customers alike.

Our Vision

Our vision is to take Indian handloom sarees to the global stage—to showcase the richness of Indian textiles and the unmatched skill of Indian weavers to audiences around the world. We aim to become a globally recognized name in Kosa silk by building international partnerships, participating in global exhibitions, and promoting our cultural heritage through storytelling, education, and fashion.

Craftsmanship & Sustainability

At the heart of KOSA BUTA lies a deep respect for nature and tradition. Our sarees are crafted using eco-friendly processes, often dyed with natural colors, and woven by hand—ensuring minimal environmental impact. We believe in slow fashion that honors both the artisan and the environment.

We work directly with local weaving communities in Chhattisgarh, ensuring fair wages, skill development, and a dignified livelihood for our artisans. By supporting KOSA BUTA, you support a larger movement to keep Indian handlooms alive, ethical, and sustainable.

Culture, Pride & Identity

A saree is not just a garment—it is a symbol of grace, strength, and cultural identity. At KOSA BUTA, we are passionate about reintroducing the saree as a versatile and empowering attire that transcends age, geography, and occasion. Through our collections, we aim to reconnect people—especially the younger generation—with the pride of wearing traditional Indian clothing in a modern context.

We also strive to educate people globally about the symbolic importance of sarees in Indian culture—their use in festivals, rituals, and celebrations—and how they represent unity in diversity across India's many regions.

Join Us

Whether you're a saree lover, a fashion enthusiast, or someone who believes in cultural preservation and ethical fashion, KOSA BUTA welcomes you. Join us on this journey as we reimagine tradition, support our weavers, and drape the world in the timeless beauty of Kosa silk.
